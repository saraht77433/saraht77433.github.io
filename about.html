About Me

Hi! I’m Sarah Theriot, a passionate data science student dedicated to turning data into actionable insights that solve real-world problems. My journey into data science grew from a deep curiosity about patterns and stories hidden in numbers, and I enjoy exploring projects that combine analysis, visualization, and predictive modeling.

I’m especially interested in environmental and conservation data, sustainability analytics, and leveraging machine learning to support decision-making in those domains. Throughout my studies and projects, I’ve gained hands-on experience with Python, R, SQL, and data visualization tools, and I’m constantly learning new techniques to improve my craft.

I believe that clear communication and ethical data use are just as important as technical skills, so I focus on creating transparent, understandable, and responsible analyses. Take a look at my portfolio to see how I apply data science to impactful challenges.
